▼

⚠️ Essential Tips
🦅 Keep bags in front — pickpockets in Gothic Quarter and Metro
🍽️ Avoid all restaurants on La Rambla — tourist traps
🕙 Locals eat dinner at 9–10pm, restaurants are lively then
📅 Plan Picasso Museum on Tue–Sun, MNAC on Tue–Sun
🚇 Get a T-Casual card (10 trips) for Metro
